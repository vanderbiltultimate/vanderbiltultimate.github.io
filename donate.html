Why Donate to Vanderbilt Ultimate?

Donating any amount helps Vanderbilt Ultimate continue to support our members and keep our club accessible to as many people as possible. Team funds are used to pay for the transportation and hotel costs of all members when traveling to tournaments.

Directions

1. Go to: https://vanderbilt.alumniq.com/giving/to/daretogrow?appealcode=A-00000478

2. Select “I'd like to search for a different option” at the bottom.

3. Type in “Vanderbilt Men’s Ultimate Frisbee Club”

4. Donate the desired amount!
